Built by students, for students.

Berean started with a simple observation: studying for the MCAT, the DAT, or any make-or-break exam is lonely, and lonely is easy to quit. Our apps turn preparation into something you can see, measure, and compete on — so showing up every day stops being a matter of willpower.
