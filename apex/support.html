Support

Apex — the focus timer that races.

Common questions

  • Do I need an account? No — racing, the season, and your history all work locally. Sign in (email or Apple) to join the live leaderboards, friends, and duels.
  • What's a Virtual Safety Car? A planned break: the field freezes, you breathe, nobody gains.
  • My rivals passed me while I was paused! That's the point — pause stops your car, not the race. Use a VSC break to freeze everyone.
  • How do duels work? Make a lobby, share the code, and the host starts a shared race clock. Everyone's cars move on everyone's track, live.
  • The lock-screen timer: Apex shows a Live Activity with a pause button — locking your phone to focus never counts against you.
  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete account (permanently removes your data).

Contact

Bugs, ideas, or anything else: admin@yourberean.com. We aim to reply within 2–3 business days.
