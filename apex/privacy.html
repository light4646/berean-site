Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Apex (“we”, “the app”) is a focus timer that turns study sessions into Formula-style races. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Account info (optional): if you sign in, your email address (or your Apple ID token if you use Sign in with Apple) and the racer name you choose.
  • Focus data: the study sessions you finish — minutes, circuit, race type, team, and month — used for your history and the leaderboards.

You can use Apex without an account; everything then stays on your device.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, browsing history, or the content of what you study.

How it's used

  • To run the app: save your races and power the monthly team, driver, and friends leaderboards.
  • Leaderboards show your racer name, team, and focus minutes to other users. We never expose your email.
  • Live duels share your live race progress (racer name, team, percent complete) with the members of a lobby you joined, for the duration of the duel only.

Where it's stored

Account data is stored with our backend provider, Supabase (PostgreSQL), protected by row-level security so only you can read or write your own records. Aggregates (team totals, top drivers) are computed server-side.

Your choices

  • Sign out any time — the app keeps working locally.
  • Delete your account and data: in-app via Settings → Delete account, which permanently removes your account and all associated data. You can also email us at the address below.

Children

Apex is a study tool intended for students; it contains no objectionable content.

Contact

Questions about your data: admin@yourberean.com
