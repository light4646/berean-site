Crown
An app by Berean

Support

Crown is a DAT study tracker — daily missions, full-length score tracking (200–600 scale, AA + TS), a content map across all six DAT sections, and leaderboards.

Contact

Email admin@yourberean.com and we'll get back to you. We aim to reply within 2–3 business days.

Common questions

  • How do I delete my account? Profile → Delete account (removes all your data).
  • Do I need an account? No — tap "Continue as guest" to use the app locally.
  • Password reset? On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?".
