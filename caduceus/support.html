Support

Need help with Caduceus — the competitive MCAT accountability app?

Contact

Email admin@yourberean.com and we'll get back to you. We aim to reply within 2–3 business days.

Common topics

  • Account sign-in / password reset: on the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?".
  • Leaderboards: rankings update as you log sessions; you control how your scores appear.
  • Score logging: log daily sessions and full-length exams from the Today tab.
  • Delete your account: open the app → Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your data.
